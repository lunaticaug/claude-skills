## üöß Contingency Planning

### Buffer Time
- Built-in buffer weeks: [Weeks X, Y, Z]
- Purpose: Catch up if behind, go deeper if ahead

### If Behind Schedule
- **Slightly behind (1-2 weeks)**:
  - Use buffer weeks to catch up
  - Add 2-3 hours per week temporarily
  - Focus on high-priority topics

- **Significantly behind (3+ weeks)**:
  - Re-evaluate timeline and possibly extend target date
  - Consider dropping lowest-priority topics
  - Increase weekly hours if sustainable
  - Seek additional help (tutor, study group)

### If Ahead of Schedule
- **Use extra time to**:
  - Deepen understanding of complex topics
  - Complete additional practice problems
  - Help others (teaching reinforces learning)
  - Explore related topics of interest
  - Take strategic breaks to avoid burnout

### If Struggling with Specific Topic
- Allocate extra 2-4 hours to that topic
- Try different resource (video vs. text)
- Seek external help (tutor, study group, online forums)
- Break topic into smaller sub-topics
- Create more practice problems for that area
